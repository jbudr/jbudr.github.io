Bachelor's thesis

Topic: "Reflections of loneliness in animated film"Work: Creative project "Kriauklė"

The aim: based on the thesis research of "Reflections of loneliness in animated film", to create a traditionally animated film that would showcase the research conclusions by conveying loneliness and similar emotions to the audience.

Tools: paper, scanner, colored pencils, watercolors, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects.
